...Magnolia Recipe...

We have a delicious suggestion for those looking for a magnolia recipe. After its fame spread from New York patisseries to the world, this dessert has become very popular in our country.
It is made with flour, baby biscuits, cream and fruit. There are variations of this recipe using different ingredients. So what is the strawberry magnolia recipe? How to make magnolia?
If you have prepared the ingredients given in our list, you can proceed to the details of the recipe.

Recipe by : Elif Atalar

Yield : 6-8 person Preparation Time : 30 minutes Cooking Time : 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 glasses of milk (1 liter)
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 100 ml cream (half a can)
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 packet of vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pack of baby biscuits
  • 1 cup hazelnut
  • Strawberry

Preparation

  1. For the custard of the magnolia, put the milk, granulated sugar, flour, eggs and liquid cream in a suitable saucepan.
  2. Let's start cooking by mixing our ingredients with a wire whisk.
  3. Let's cook the custard by stirring until it thickens and becomes thick and then turn off the stove.
  4. After adding vanilla into it and mixing it, let's take the custard into a suitable mixing bowl.
  5. While our custard is waiting, cover it with cling film. In this way, we will prevent it from crusting.
  6. On the other hand, let's put the baby biscuits and roasted hazelnuts in the rondo and quickly pass through the rondo.
  7. Beat the cooled custard with a mixer for about two minutes. Now we can start filling the dessert into the mugs we will present.
  8. Now let's first place the hazelnut biscuit mixture on the bottom of the mugs.
  9. Then place sliced strawberries on the sides of our mugs.
  10. After arranging the strawberries, pour the custard into the mugs. Smooth the top of the custard with the back of the spoon.
  11. Finally, place the hazelnut biscuit mixture on the custard again. Decorate our magnolias with sliced strawberries.
  12. In this way, after resting our desserts in the refrigerator for at least 2-3 hours, our strawberry magnolias are ready to serve.
